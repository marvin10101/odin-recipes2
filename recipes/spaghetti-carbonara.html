Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, comforting, and easy to make!

This version stays true to the traditional method and only takes about 20 minutes from start to finish.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water.
  2. In a pan, fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta and quickly mix it with the pancetta, egg mixture, and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately with extra parmesan if desired.
